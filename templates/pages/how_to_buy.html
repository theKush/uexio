Buy with UEXIO

UEXIO makes it easy for students to exchange textbooks

UEXIO connects university students with other students buying or selling textbooks. When you sign up, your email address is school-verified, then you will have access to the online marketplace. Buyers are allowed to comment on the listing while browsing to get the best deal from the seller. Users will be able to rate each other so that buyers can find the most valued seller. UEXIO is different from other textbook sites in that we allow the seller to include their class notes and supplemental materials for download. Sellers will pay commission (smaller sales fee compared to competitors) of course. A PayPal email is also allowed for purchases. With UEXIO, users will feel safe browsing the market and can easily do business offline too.
