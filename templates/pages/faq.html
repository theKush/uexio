Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I sell other items besides textbooks?

UEXIO may extend the merchandise list out to allow other school-related materials such as calculators, backpacks, etc but for now it is a marketplace for textbooks only.

As a seller, what is the point of uploading additional files with a new listing?

Any user who creates a listing may sell their own notes along with their textbook. This is where UEXIO excels from the competitors because it allows the seller to put a higher value on their product. Also, the buyer may download the files included with the purchase and use them as they like when they take the course. It's a win-win!

If I am selling, where do I enter the price I am charging for shipping?

The shipping cost should be included in the product price (if any), since UEXIO is only within the U.S. So whenever a seller creates a new listing he/she includes the shipping cost in the price of the product if he wants to.

Where will my purchase be shipped to?

Once a sale is made, the seller must ship to the PayPal address of the buyer. If you want to ship to a different address, you can email the seller under the "My Purchases" tab on your profile page.

How do coupons work?

This is another feature UEXIO is proud of having because it will help the seller make a discounted price available to any buyer with the coupon code provided to them via advertisement or from the seller directly. It adds extra incentive for the buyer looking for a good price. To add a coupon code to one of your products, go to "Active Listings" on your profile. Click the "Coupons" button on the right of the product, enter the code and discount amount, then just press "Update". You now have a discount available for your product!

If my book hasn't shipped within the estimated shipping period, what should I do?

If you have already tried reaching out to the seller via email and you don't receive a timely reply, please contact the administrators immediately through our Contact page. Worst case, we will refund your money back and ban the seller from UEXIO.
